CHAPTER 1 THE FIRST MEET

2030, in front of the gate of Lonis’ School for Disabled Women, there was coming a freshwoman.

This upset girl was wearing tidy school uniforms, with her black, curly hair spreading in the wind. Her glasses was reflecting the pleasing environment around this special school – yellow 枫树叶 stacked on the ground, and a small red colored road could be seen among these leaves.

Today is her first day to go to this school. She was carrying her luggages and bags herself.

“Good luck to yourself, Mizui.” she said to herself.

For what she said this is that she was forced to go here by her parents. As an income girl, lots of unhappy things would be happened to her. She said this to relief the uneasiness in her deep heart.

It’s the new semester of fall, and she came in to the assigned classroom, which was noticed to her in the smartphone email. While she was coming into the classroom, she found a girl who was in green, not like others who were wearing blue school uniforms. The girl was watching the scenes outside the windows, and it seemed like she was thinking about something deeply or just taking a nap. After checking in, Mizui was assigned to the seat beside the girl on the sheet, and then she was told to put her luggages in the dormitories.

Miss Mizui carried her luggages towards the dormitory building and went upstairs to find her room.

“Let me have a look… hmm… Room 210, this one, alright.”Mizui looked around, and went to a room in the end of the corridor.

She pushed the door and walked in.

There was a weird smell spreading out, and then she found a girl sleeping in a bed, surrounded by many snacks and electronic devices.

“Oh my…”she was about to scream.

“I wonder if it’s a murder…?”Mizui told herself, with a strong sense of astonishment.

But the girl turned her body upside down, and opened her eyes a little, then fell asleep again. It seemed like she’s just extremely sleepy.

While Mizui was setting up her daily stuff, she observed the room and her roommate ocassionally. This girl looked kinda familiat and she recognized her as that white-grey hair girl she just had met in the classroom.

Mizui went out to check out the structure of her new dorm room. The sheet stuck on the door told her that there would only 2 people in this room, and her only roommate’s name was Yukimi Yuzuki(her name seemed to be on there for a long time and the bottom part of the sheet was newer)
